My Read Me for my I3E Assignment.

Here are the Key Controls for my assignment. The Key controls is ONLY for Keyboard
Walk - W
Run - Hold Shift
Jump - Spacebar
Move Left - A
Move Right - D
Walk Backwards - S
Interact - E
Menu Panel - Esc Key

There are 3 Parts of the Map. The Fallen Pillars, The Maze & Gas Chamber and the Finale. 

The Fallen Pillars essential makes the user have to jump to fallen pillars without falling into the lava while collecting the coins. There are a total of 4 Coins Including the secret Area. The lava in the Fallen Pillar room would instantly kill the player upon touching it. The secret area would require the player to open the starter door and jump over to another platform while trying not to hit the wall.

Upon finishing the lava obstacle, The player would find 1 Coin to collect and would see the door leading to the gas room on the left. The Gas Room requires the gas mask item as the green gas in the room slowly damages the player's 10HP every second. The maze has multiple lava area and only to the end of the maze would the player find the gas mask. Obtaining the gas mask would allow the player's hp to not get affected by the gas room. The Maze has 2 coins,and the gas chamber has 1 Coin. After passing through the gas room, the player would come to the finale which is another obstacle that the player would have to scale. It is slightly much harder to clear the obstacle as there are multiple pillars that is quite far apart so timing their jumps are important. Dying in the game would force the player to restart from the start. There is a coin on one of the falling pillars which the player has to climb up a pillar. After completing the obstacles, the player would find the last coin situated at the end. After finding ALL Ten of the coins, the player wins!

The player would need to run the game on Unity by pressing the play button.

The platform required to run the game is a desktop a 64-bit Windows 10 or Windows 11 are required, as for apple, an Intel or Apple Silicon macOS 10.14 or newer. Since my game is a 3D Unity Game, it would at least require a system with a CPU: Dual-core 2.3 GHz or better.RAM: 4 GB.GPU: DirectX 10/11-capable (for Windows), Metal-capable (for macOS), or OpenGL 3.2+ / Vulkan capable for Linux

In terms of Bugs or Limitations, there's a very low chance the player would somehow be able to collect 2 coins when they only collected it once. Therefore having the game end only when just collecting 9 coins. There is also another situation where when the player needs to jump to the top of pillars to collect a coin, player might be stuck between the roof. I did decrease the pillar's height so that it pefectly fits, However there's still a slight chance to get stuck.
